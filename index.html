Burgbichl
XR technologies present unique solutions to communicate Cultural Heritage knowledge to broad audiences. They enhance Cultural Heritage conservation and education by creating engaging learning environments employing storytelling attributes, active learning features, and Serious Gaming concepts. This research develops an outdoor, AR prototype for the archaeological excavation site on Burgbichl in close cooperation with the team leading the archaeological intervention. The prototype allows users to become archaeologists and interactively explore the site.
Green Wall
2024
Green Wall

The Great Green Wall (GGW) of the Sahara and Sahel Initiative adapted by the African Union inspires our game idea. The initiative aims to ultimately stop desertification and landdegradation in the Sahel zone by implementing a mosaic of various locally adapted projects.These projects range from sustainable agriculture and agroforestry to reforestation, watermanagement and soil measures such as erosion control. Local communities implement, maintain and innovate those projects as required by the local circumstances.

We incorporate these initiatives, methods and values into a strategic community andeconomy building game. Players manage and guide a community faced with the drastic consequences of climate change and land degradation. Players need to implement agricultural techniques that are able to withstand the harsh environment to secure food and a living for their community. In time, they will build a network with other communities to tackle larger projects and reclaim land from the desert.

Battleship VR
Battleship VR
Battleship VR is a reinterpretation of the classic board game Battleship, using a QR cube to interact with the game board. The game stars with a Placement Phase, where players hide their ships. Afterward, they automatically enter the Shooting Phase, where players compete against the computer's strategy. To navigate the game, players have to print and assemble the QR Marker Cube.
